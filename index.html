About Me

Greetings Web Surfer!

My name is Kendall Zettlmeier. I am a developer and a lover of all things technology. I currently am an Senior Software Engineer at GE Healthcare located in Chicago, IL where I work on a .NET WPF application that monitors the mother and fetus during birth.

I am someone who can talk about technology all day long, especially when it comes to new development techniques and frameworks. I am always looking for something new to learn.

One thing I love is a challenge, and that is something that I am always looking for as a developer. I was lucky to have a chance to be the Head of Technology at a startup called YouSwoop at the young age of 21. Being in that position definitely opened my eyes for how you always need to be on the lookout for new technologies, because in order to get ahead of your competitors, you yourself have to have something that they don't have. Ever since then, I always look for something that I don't know and I take on the challenge of learning it and finding where I can implement it in my future and existing projects.
